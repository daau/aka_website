Our Story

AKA is an Asian fusion restaurant brand whose cuisine combines a host of unique recipes with an equally distinctive dining experience. Having sourced ingredients and culinary ideas from various parts of Asia and elsewhere in the world, AKA is truly a journey for the palate that can be explored in one place.

The restaurant’s signature entrees are served on a 200 degree Celsius hot plate, which allow the food to be cooked to the customer’s preference, guarantee a standard of freshness, and take flavours to a whole new level. Guests can also customize their dishes with an array of ingredients that are optionally added on to each sizzling meal. Exclusive appetizers, sauces and cocktails make the dining experience at AKA that much more unique.

AKA is about embracing different cultures and emboldening individuals, all while preparing amazing meals, drinks, and experiences along the way.
